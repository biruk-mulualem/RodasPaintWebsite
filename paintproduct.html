RODAS PAINTS

RODAS PAINTS is a key product of SUPER DOUBLE "T" GENERAL TRADING PLC, one of Ethiopia's leading paint manufacturers. Known for its superior quality, Rodas Paint has become the most famous paint brand in the country. Produced in the state-of-the-art factory of SUPER DOUBLE "T" General Trading Plc, it is widely used in construction and other applications setting the standard for modern, high-quality paint products in Ethiopia. With advanced technology, skilled labor, and a commitment to excellence, Rodas Paints continues to lead the paint industry in Ethiopia.

SUPER DOUBLE "T" General Trading Plc has a well-equipped plant with the latest technology and machinery and a standard quality control laboratory to deliver undisputable quality products. It also constantly conducts exploratory research to upgrade and fabricate new products and to create market opportunity. SUPER DOUBLE "T" General Trading Plc has expanded its capacity, developed a skilled workforce and introduces integrated technologies, to remain globally up-to-date. Today, RODAS paint is the prime choice of many Customers and it has good network of dealers and distributers all across the country and is competing for the same goal internationally in the near future.
