RODAS SUPER

Interior & Exterior Wall Paint

Preparation- Before painting, the surface must be clean and free from old,loose paints,water soluble and chalking paints must be removed throughly.

Application-Easily applied by brush,roller or spray gun.

Packing

RODAS MICA

Interior & Exterior Wall Paint

Preparation- The surface to be coated must be thoroughly dry. Remove loose and powdery residues if any. Repair cracks with suitable filler and allow to dry hard.

Application-For new or "green" work, apply one coat of Rodas Mica and allow to dry before applying a second coat. It is easily applied by brush or roller or any other suitable device.

Drying Time

2-3hrs

Coverage

15-17 sq.m per Liter

Packing

4 Liter

Dilution

use water 8-10% of the paint

Color Range

Unlimited Color Range

Caution

DON'T mix Rodas super with other paints
