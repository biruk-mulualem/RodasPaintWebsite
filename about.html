INTRODUCTION

Super Double T General Trading Plc was eastablished as private Limited Company business entity in 1999 EC and as holding company there are several business entities under its name. The company engaged in different business activities namely manufacturing, import, contracting whole sales and retail sales.

Super Fiber Glass Products manufacturing, Rodas Paint Industry, Super water proofing works, Super solar energy technology, Super Rubber Seal Factory and Pomy Marble Factory are the main sectors under Super Double "T" General Trading plc.

The diversification of our products, manufacturing of marketable products, whole sale and distribution of imported items is based on the detail market research and experience on the business. The company increases its performance from time to time by additional advantages of incorporating human resources, market assessments, and capability of selecting niche markets.
