POMY MARBLE FACTORY

POME MARBLE, a brand under SUPER DOUBLE "T" GENERAL TRADING PLC, is a leading Ethiopian manufacturer of high-quality marble and dolomite products used in industries like paint, tiles, soaps, and detergents. The factory uses advanced technology and machinery to ensure top-quality products and focuses on research and development to expand capacity and integrate new technologies.

What is Marble?
Marble is a metamorphic rock formed when limestone is exposed to heat and pressure, mainly composed of calcite (CaCO3) and minerals like quartz, pyrite, and iron oxides. During metamorphism, calcite recrystallizes into interlocking crystals. Dolomitic marble forms from dolostone under similar conditions.

What is Dolomite?
Dolomite is a harder, more brittle stone with high magnesium content. It is used as ornamental stone, concrete aggregate, and in magnesium oxide production. It also serves in petroleum reservoirs and base metal deposits and can replace limestone in iron and steel production. In horticulture, it lowers soil acidity and provides magnesium. Pome Marble Factory produces a variety of products from these marble and dolomite materials for multiple industries.
