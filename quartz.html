RODAS QUARTZ GRAFFITI

Interior & Exterior Wall

PREPARATION- The surface must be cleaned from dirt and mixed well before use. Prior to applying Rodas Quartz, it is advisable to apply Rodas Primer. Use a plastic or metal trowel for application.

ADVANTAGE- It provides a shiny finish, prevents the wall from cracking, is easily washable, and offers resistance to extreme weather conditions and water.

Drying Time

40-45 minutes at a normal tempreture

Coverage

6-7sq.m per 25kg

Packing
