SUPER SOLAR ENERGY

Super Hani is a brand under Super Double "T" General Trading PLC, offering high-quality solar solutions. We import, deliver, and install Solar Power Water Heaters, Photovoltaic (PV) Solar Panels, and Electric Water Heaters to help residential and commercial clients save money, reduce their carbon footprint, and enhance energy efficiency.

1. Solar Power (Photovoltaic)
Photovoltaic (PV) Modules convert sunlight into electricity. Simple, effective, and durable, these systems have no moving parts and can power your home, charge batteries, or feed energy into the grid. Benefits:
Save Money: Solar power is now cheaper than grid electricity. Installing solar systems protects against rising electricity costs and delivers long-term savings. Environmental Impact: Solar energy generates zero emissions, helping reduce your carbon footprint.

2. Solar Water Heaters
Solar Water Heating (SWH) uses solar energy to heat water, providing a cost-effective and sustainable way to generate hot water. Benefits:
Reduce Energy Bills: Solar water heaters lower heating costs, which account for 25% of average energy bills. Eco-Friendly: They reduce carbon emissions and reliance on fossil fuels. Increase Home Value: These systems improve your home's energy efficiency and may increase property value.

3. Electric Water Heaters
Electric Water Heaters use electricity to heat water on demand. They are compact, efficient, and simple to install. Advantages:
Space-Saving: Small size compared to traditional storage tanks. Cost-Effective: Affordable, efficient, and easy to install. On-Demand Hot Water: Enjoy an endless supply of hot water with instant heating.
