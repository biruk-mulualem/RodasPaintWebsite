RODAS ALKYD VARNISH

Synthetic Gloss Varnish

Preparation- The surface of the wood must be clean, dry and free from dust, grease. Rough spots must be smoothed using sand paper. paints.

Application- Apply one coat of Rodas Alkyd Varnish and allow to dry for 10-12 hours & then apply a second coat. Keep the container closed when not in use and avoid from heat and open flame.

Drying Time

10-12 hr

Packing

4 Liter

Dilution

Can be diluted with turpentine

RODAS POLY VARNISH

Used- for all wood surface, it is particularly designed for high grade furniture, parquet floors, where high gloss finish and water resistance is required.

Preparation- the surface must be clean dry and free from wax and grease. Use sand paper to eliminate rough spots to secure a smooth surface..

Application- apply one coat polyurethane varnish and allow drying for about 4-5hrs then apply a second coat. It can be applied by brush or spry gun.

Caution- keep the container closed when not in use and avoid heat open flame.

Packing

4 Liter
