RODAS EPOXY PAINT

Rodas epoxy paint is a two component paint system

Instruction for use:-- Prepare surface very carefully ensuring all dust, dirt, grease oil etc... has been removed completely. Mix part A (paint) and part B (hardener) well before use. Mix the parts together well. Paint the primer first then after 24 hour apply 2 coast of the topcoat. Make sure you used the paint completely with in 2 hours after mixing with hardener.

Packing

4 Liter

Dilution

With Hardner


RODAS POLYESTER PUTTY

Used:-- to repair damaged body panel on vehicles. It is used to fill and repair the damaged parts of all cars, buses, any cracked metal surface and flower pots. It is very easy for sanding and dries quickly.

Instruction for use:--The surfaces to be coated must be clear, dry, and possibly roughened to improve adhesion. Depending on the room temperature, add 2-3% of the catalyzer by volume and mix the components carefully to ensure a homogeneous blend.

USED FOR

Damaged Car Body and Metal Surface
