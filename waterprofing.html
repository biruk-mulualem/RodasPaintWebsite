Super Koki Waterproofing

Super Koki, a brand under Super Double "T" General Trading PLC, is the leading waterproof engineering company in the country. We offer top-notch waterproofing solutions to protect your property from water damage, ensuring long-lasting results and structural integrity.

What is Waterproofing?
Waterproofing is the process of making a structure resistant to water, preventing moisture from entering and causing damage. This is achieved through membranes and coatings, which safeguard the contents inside and protect the building's structural strength.


Our Services

Cementitious Waterproofing: Protective moisture barriers for cement walls and floors using epoxy or latex-based mixes.
Membrane Waterproofing: Polymer-modified asphalt emulsion membranes for external wall waterproofing,

Our Process

Preparation: We start by repairing cracks and holes to ensure a smooth surface before applying the waterproofing materials.
Application: Our expert technicians apply high-quality membranes.
